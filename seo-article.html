What to do if an investment stops paying

Practical steps for investors — neutral guidance. (This page is not legal advice.)

Overview

If you are an investor in Concept Capital Group LTD and have experienced missed payments or reduced communications, this page provides neutral, practical steps and resources to help you coordinate with others. This is educational and community-focused — not legal or financial advice.

Immediate steps

  1. Gather documentation: contracts, payment records, emails, receipts, and any communication from the company.
  2. Record dates and amounts for missed payments and keep screenshots of any relevant pages.
  3. Attempt direct contact via official channels (email and website contact forms) and log attempts.

Community coordination

Joining our WhatsApp group helps coordinate actions, share templates (e.g., demand letters), and pool knowledge of local regulation or lawyers others have used. Return to the community hub or refresh this guide.

Legal & regulatory checks

Depending on your local jurisdiction, there are often regulatory bodies or agencies that handle investment fraud or unlicensed financial activities. Consider contacting local regulators, a consumer protection agency, or a qualified solicitor who specialises in investor recovery.

Template resources (examples)

We keep sample templates in the group for demand letters and data requests — share and adapt with care.

Staying safe online

Avoid scams: never share private keys, bank PINs, or passwords. When coordinating with others, validate identities and exercise caution when sharing documents that contain sensitive personal data.
