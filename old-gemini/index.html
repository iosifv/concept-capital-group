FCA Takes Action Against Concept Capital Group

¿

The Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) has initiated High Court proceedings against Concept Capital Group, alleging it was operating an unauthorised collective investment scheme. The FCA has obtained undertakings to freeze the firm's assets. This action raises serious concerns about the company's operations and the security of invested funds. Many are now questioning if Concept Capital Group is a scam or a Ponzi scheme.
